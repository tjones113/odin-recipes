Recipe for Spagetti

Note: results may vary from images shown

Ingredients

  • Spagetti
  • Tomato Ketchup
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the Spagetti in a pan of water, for about 10 minutes
  2. Drain the Spagetti of water and add to a bowl
  3. Add Ketchup and stir well
  4. Sprinkle some grated cheese over the top
  5. Season to taste and serve
