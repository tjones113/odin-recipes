Lasagne

Recipe for Vegetarian Lasagne

Note: results may vary from images shown

Ingredients

  • Lasagne Sheets
  • Tomato Ketchup
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the lasagne sheets for about 10 minutes
  2. Remove the lasagne sheets from the water and drain
  3. Place a lasagne sheet onto a plate
  4. Add Ketchup
  5. Add cheese
  6. Add another lasagne sheet
  7. repeat this process until your last sheet
  8. On top of the last sheet, add the remaining cheese
  9. Microwave for 30 seconds to melt the cheese
  10. Season to taste and Serve
