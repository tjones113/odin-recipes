Ravioli

Recipe for Ravioli

Note: results may vary from images shown

Ingredients

  • Ravioli Sheets
  • Tomato Ketchup
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the Ravioli sheets in a pan of water for about 10 minutes
  2. Drain the Ravioli sheets of water and place onto a plate
  3. For each parcel, add a small amount of ketchup
  4. Add a sprinkle of grated cheese
  5. Fold over the ravioli sheet in half
  6. Press firmly over the edges of the parcel with a fork to seal
  7. Repeat until all parcels have been sealed
  8. Season to taste and Serve
